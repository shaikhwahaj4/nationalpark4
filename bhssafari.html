Choose Your Behind-The-Scenes Safari:



PLATYPUSSES AND
FRIENDS

Ages 3 and up87$ AND UP

Offered daily; 90 minutes

Visit behind the scenes at Walkabout Australia! On this 90-minute guided tour, you will get an exclusive behind-the-scenes look at the Park's newest exhibit and learn what makes the platypus one of nature's most unusual mammals. You will hear about the work we are doing to help these animals and learn what you can do to help their wild counterparts.

Participants must be 3 years of age or older. Children ages 15 and younger must be accompanied by a paid adult.


ELEPHANTS AND
FRIENDS

Ages 3 and up87$ AND UP

Offered daily; 90 minutes

Go on safari behind the scenes at Elephant Valley! On this guided tour, you will learn about the amazing ways we care for our animals and how what we do here can help their wild counterparts. You will enjoy viewing Elephant Valley from a private viewing area while discovering what it takes to care for our one-of-a-kind African elephant herd.


TIGERS AND
FRIENDS

Ages 3 and up87$ AND UP

Offered daily; 90 minutes

Get a rare look at Tiger Trail to learn how we care for our magnificent tigers! Your adventure will include a walk through Tiger Trail, and a visit to the tiger habitat area. Up to 60 minutes of walking or standing is required.


RHINOS AND
FRIENDS

Ages 3 and up87$ AND UP

Offered daily; 90 minutes

Go behind the scenes at the Nikita Kahn Rhino Rescue Center—a 3.5-acre state-of-the-art facility dedicated to the conservation of white rhinos, with a focus on assisted reproductive techniques highlighting our fight to end extinction. In addition, you’ll journey around the Safari Park’s unique multispecies field enclosures. Up to 60 minutes of walking or standing is required.


ROOS AND MATES

Ages 3 and up87$ AND UP

Offered daily; 90 minutes

Travel Down Under to visit some of your favorite hopping marsupials at the Safari Park's Walkabout Australia. While visiting the Walkabout, you will learn how the Park provides our animals with top-notch care, and view their habitats. About 90 minutes of walking or standing is required.


CHEETAHS AND
FRIENDS

Ages 3 and up87$ AND UP

Offered daily; 90 minutes

Venture to Benbough Amphitheater to see one of our cheetahs! Learn more about cheetah conservation and how we care for the Safari Park’s cheetahs, and enjoy a guided look at some of the Safari Park’s other animals.
