KID'S CARAVAN
ADVENTURE

Ages 8 and up121$ AND UP

2 Hours

Calling all superheroes! Animals have some incredible super powers that help them survive—and YOU can be a superhero for wildlife!

This special tour experience—designed for children ages 6 and up, and their families—will help kids graduate from Wildlife Hero Academy and learn about our animal superheroes, and how they can make a difference for them. Participants will board a Caravan Safari truck to visit animals that live in our expansive South Africa and East Africa savanna habitats and play games to earn a Wildlife Hero badge!

Offered Saturdays and Sundays, year round.

NOTE: Take a Caravan Safari Adventure before the Park opens! Offered on select dates at 8:45 a.m. Please check availability.
